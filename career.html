Biologists study humans, animals, plants, and the environment they live in. To become a biologist you would need a bachelors degree in biology or one of its specialized fields such as micro biology, botany, or bacteriology.
The skills you would need to become a biologist are:

  • Knowledge of biology
  • Communications skills
  • Research skills
  • Math skills

A biologist salary is ranges from 46k-91k yearly
